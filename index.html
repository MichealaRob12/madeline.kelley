Professional Interests

I have experience conducting lab experiments in wind tunnels and flumes, field experiments in coastal, riverine, and desert environments, as well as modeling work.

My work focuses on landscape mechanisms and the quantitative relationships between physical processes.

About Me

When not working, I'm playing outside. I love exploring beautiful and diverse landscapes, both near and far. My partner and I are frequently out with our dogs biking, hiking, fishing, camping, and rafting/kayaking.

Service

Having participated in several service projects, I am proud to showcase all of my community's achievements.

I enjoy dedicating time to service and helping others acheive their goals within the scientific community.

Teaching

Teaching others about fluvial processes is my passion.

Participating in talks and seminars about sediment transport within the scientific community gives me an opportunity to share my findings with others and seek out new opportunites.

Education

I have had the incredible opportunity to work with amazing scientists and engineers from different schools across the US.

Traveling for educational and professional opportunities has allowed me to connect with many incredible people.
